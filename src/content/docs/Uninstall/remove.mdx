---
title: Uninstall Tomodachi Life HD
---

Right-click your game in Citra's Game List and click on `Open Custom Texture Location`
![1](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FIREXDF/TLHD-Docs/main/public/img/uninstall/1.png)

Now Delete the `Tomodachi Life HD` folder.

![2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/FIREXDF/TLHD-Docs/main/public/img/uninstall/2.png)

We're sad that you got to uninstall it but thanks for trying it out anyways, support is always appreciated !
